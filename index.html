what we're playing this month!

Small World

Small World is inhabited by a zany cast of characters such as dwarves, wizards, amazons, giants, orcs and even humans; who use their troops to occupy territory and conquer adjacent lands. Players vie for area-control of a world that is simply too small to accommodate them all.

Ticket to Ride

Build your tracks across Europe in this fast-paced and strategic board game. Connect iconic European cities across a map of the early 20th-century Europe and build your train routes to earn points

Herd Mentality

Flip over a question, grab a pencil and follow the crowd. Try to figure out what everyone else would write! If your answer is in the majority, you win cows. If your answer is the odd one out, you'll be given the Pink Cow and you'll have no chance of winning...until you can moo-ve it on to someone else.

Color Brain

Can you remember what colour the five Olympic rings are? How about the colour of the four Teletubbies? Or the lid on a jar of marmite? Play the correct colours and get points, as long as someone gets it wrong. First to reach 10 points, wins!
